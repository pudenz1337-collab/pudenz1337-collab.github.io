💡 Tirzepatide + Muscle Building Rules
✓
Prioritize protein even when not hungry. Tirzepatide suppresses appetite — have an Oikos Pro shake if eating feels hard.
✓
Don't skip meals entirely. Muscle needs a steady amino acid supply. Aim for 4–5 small protein meals vs 1–2 large ones.
✓
Eat within 30–60 min after training. A protein source immediately post-workout is critical for muscle building.
✓
Stay in a moderate deficit. Too aggressive = muscle loss. 300–500 kcal below maintenance lets fat come off while muscle stays.
✓
Hydrate more on injection days. Tirzepatide can cause GI effects — extra water helps.
